About

The Junkyard is a place for storing dumb crap you don't want to look at anymore. We'll take pretty much anything short of radioactive materials! (Our Sister Company handles all of those!)


The Junkyard also provides some wonderful opportunites to find some neat crap, so feel free to browse our vast stores of junk!

Contribute

If you got some junk to throw on the pile we'll be happy to take it, as long as it meets our criteria below.

  • Must be an eyesore
  • Must have at least one sharp edge
  • No less than 50% surface rust
  • Could potentially be another man's treasure

If your dumb crap meets at least one of the criteria(its a Junkyard, we aren't very strict.) bring it on by!

Jump on In!

If you are interested in browsing our wares come on by any day of the week at pretty much any time! Prior to admission you'll need to fill out 3-5 liability waivers and watch a short video titled "Junk Monsters and you", but don't let that scare you! The Junk Monsters are slow and quite dull.

The Junkyard is not responsible for any injury, fatalities or subsequent re-animations that may occur. If you fail to bring a magic sword to protect yourself that is really on you.

I made this thing to mess around and practice my coding in the year 2018.
